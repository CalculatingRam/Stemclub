STEM education helps learners develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, which are essential for enabling them to think creatively and independently and apply complex concepts and ideas to help students develop and improve their technology skills. By participating in activities such as coding, programming, web design, and digital content creation, students can gain hands-on experience with various technology tools.

The main goal of this club is to run various ICT-related programs that help students to become more skilled and academically knowledgeable in their field. Students are benefited from the club's various platforms to obtain experience in the field of information and technology.
